## Part 3: Critical Habitability Windows

### Window 1: Ocean Evaporation Threshold

**Deadline:** ~1 billion years from now

**Physics:**
- Increased solar luminosity (+10%) drives runaway greenhouse effect
- Water vapor (potent greenhouse gas) creates feedback loop
- Stratospheric water vapor photodissociated by UV radiation
- Hydrogen escapes to space; oxygen reacts with surface rocks
- Process is **irreversible** once triggered

**Consequence:** Earth becomes a second Venus—scorched, dry, 450°C surface

**Action Window:** Evacuate Earth **before** this threshold (by Year +900 million recommended)

---

### Window 2: Livable Surface Deadline

**Deadline:** ~500-750 million years from now

**Physics:**
- At +5% luminosity, global temperatures rise significantly
- Ice caps melt permanently
- Sea levels rise 60+ meters
- Equatorial regions become uninhabitable (50°C+ daily averages)
- Agricultural collapse in most regions

**Consequence:** Surface life possible only at poles; mass die-offs; civilization stress

**Action Window:** Begin underground migration OR orbital sunshade deployment by Year +500 million

---

### Window 3: Complex Life Extinction

**Deadline:** ~1-1.5 billion years from now

**Physics:**
- Extreme heat + moisture loss kills most multicellular life
- Photosynthesis becomes impossible (water/CO₂ balance disrupted)
- Food chains collapse
- Only extremophile bacteria survive in deep underground aquifers

**Consequence:** Biosphere collapses; 99.9%+ species extinction

**Action Window:** Preserve genetic diversity / seed banks by Year +900 million
