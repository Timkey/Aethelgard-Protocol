## Executive Summary

The Planetary Exodus project currently contains a fundamental timeline inconsistency that undermines scientific credibility:

**The Problem:**
- Documents present a 5,000-year mission (2026-6700) with urgent "flee the dying Sun" framing
- Scientific reality: Sun won't threaten Earth for ~1 billion years
- This creates a credibility gap that needs correction

**The Solution:**
A comprehensive four-document framework that:
1. Identifies the specific inconsistencies
2. Proposes a scientifically accurate dual-timeline approach
3. Provides a systematic review strategy
4. Delivers ready-to-use correction templates
