## Key Insights From This Process

1. **Solar Physics Reality:**
   - Ocean evaporation: ~1 billion years
   - Red giant expansion: ~5 billion years
   - 10% brightness increase per billion years

2. **Why Start in 2026 if No Urgency?**
   - Capability window (industrial civilization might not persist)
   - Knowledge preservation (lose critical tech every 1,000-5,000 years on average)
   - Test run philosophy (prove systems work when you have time to fix them)
   - Civilizational continuity (multi-generational projects anchor cultures)

3. **Phase Zero is NOT Evacuation:**
   - It's testing: asteroid deflection, lunar mass drivers, Hive prototypes
   - It's simulation: navigation algorithms, life support systems
   - It's validation: proving concepts work before they're needed
   - It's insurance: build capability now, use it in 500 million years

4. **The Paradox of Deep Time:**
   - If we wait until we "need" to evacuate (500M years), we'll have lost the capability
   - Bronze Age → Space Age took 5,000 years
   - Dark Age → Renaissance took 1,000 years  
   - We lose and rebuild knowledge constantly
   - **Solution:** Build capability when civilization is strong, maintain it across deep time
